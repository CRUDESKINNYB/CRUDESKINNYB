1: Client Ideas and Drawings

In the first stage we liaise with the client about their design ideas and wishes. Using sketches, photos or just by talking to us, we can get a clearer idea of exact design you have in mind.

Using the information we have been given. We can make a comprehensive virtual 3D model to show the client. This gives us a better idea of the look and feel of the piece, and to make sure we are on the same page.

2: Constructing the Piece

After we discuss and finalise the design, we take the clients ideas into the workshop. We will pick out the best pieces of timber to use from the woodyard using sustainable materials.

Once in our workshop; we will handcraft the piece/s of furniture to the design specifications; updating the client along our journey as the piece develops.

3: Finished Piece

Once the piece of furniture is complete; we have it professionally photographed to feature on our website.

We go on to check that the quality is what we and client expect from Skinner Designs. At this stage the piece is ready to be shipped or collected.
