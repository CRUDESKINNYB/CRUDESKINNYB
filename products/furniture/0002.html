Z-chair - Mahogany, White oak

Based on the famous 1950's Selig Z-Chair by Poul Jensen, this pair of chairs revitalizes the iconic style of Danish design.

These chairs have a striking elegant look and feel to suit a huge range of interior design styles.

In-particularly they have a solid White Oak core which runs through both sides of the chair creating a unique pair of chairs.

Finished in High Gloss Varnish; furnished with two comfy 'Duck Egg' blue foam cushions to achieve the 1950's look and feel.
