Made To Order - American/European Cherry

A beautiful hand crafted end grain chopping board available in a multitude of sizes and styles.

Available options:

-

Classic Style

Our classic Style features straight sides with ergonomic recesses (handles) to help lift the board.

-

Japanese Style

Our Japanese style boards offers a elegant look with sleek tapered edges.

-

Sizes available (40mm thick)

Extra Large: 500mm x 400mm

Large: 500mm x 320mm

Medium: 400mm x 220mm

Small: 300mm x 220mm

-

If you would an alternate size or thickness that is not listed here please contact me via email to discuss your needs.

Sanded to a smooth glossy surface and treated with food safe mineral oil to protect the board.

For further protection a special blend on mineral oil and beeswax is applied to the surface. This makes the end grain chopping board more durable, more resilient to water as well as enhancing the glossy finish.

Mineral Oil and Chopping Board Wax is also available to buy to ensure your board stays in the best condition.
