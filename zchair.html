Mahogany and White Oak Z-Chairs

Based on the famous 1950's Selig Z-Chair by Poul Jensen, this pair of chairs revitalizes the iconic style of Danish design. Made from 1950s era, reclaimed Mahogany timber, it features a solid White Oak core which runs through both sides of the chair. These chairs have a striking elegant look and feel to suit a huge range of interior design styles. Finished in High Gloss Varnish; furnished with two comfy 'Duck Egg' blue foam cushions to achieve the 1950's look and feel.

Currently one of a kind... Or two.

Sold as a pair.
