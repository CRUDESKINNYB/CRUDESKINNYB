Mahogany and White Oak Z-Chairs

Based on the famous 1950's Selig Z-Chair by Poul Jensen, this pair of chairs revitalizes the iconic style of Danish design. Made from 1950s era reclamied Mahogany timber, it features a solid White Oak core which runs through both sides of the chair. These chairs have a striking elegant look and feel and suit a huge range of interoir design styles. Finished in High Gloss Varnish; furnished with two comfy 'Duck Egg' blue foam cushions to achive the 1950's look and feel.
